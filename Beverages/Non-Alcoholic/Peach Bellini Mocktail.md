# Peach Bellini Mocktail

**Glass Type: Champagne Flute**

## Ingredients

| Ingredient                                                                                        | Quantity  |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------- |
| Peach Nectar or Puree, Chilled                                                                    | 2 Tbsp    |
| Ginger Ale, Chilled                                                                               | 3/8 Cup   |
| Fresh Berries                                                                                     | 1/8 Cup   |

## Instructions

1. Combine Peach Nectar and Ginger Ale in glass.
2. Float berries on top.
